# 🐝 Beehive Of AI

**Personal Computers Working Together as One Powerful AI**

---

## What Is This?

Beehive Of AI is a platform where people can join teams of home computers that work together to solve AI tasks for companies.

- **Workers** earn money by sharing their computer's AI processing power with the network.
- **Companies** get affordable AI by using the distributed network instead of expensive cloud AI services.

Think of it like Airbnb — but instead of renting out a spare room, you rent out your computer's idle AI power. Your computer does the work while you sleep, and you get paid for it.

---

## The Beehive Metaphor

We named everything after a beehive because a beehive is nature's perfect example of distributed intelligence — thousands of simple creatures working together to accomplish something none of them could do alone.

| Term | What It Means |
|------|--------------|
| **Queen Bee** | A team manager whose computer splits big AI tasks into small sub-tasks and combines the results back together |
| **Worker Bee** | A home computer owner who processes individual AI sub-tasks using their local AI model |
| **Hive** | A team of computers (one Queen Bee + many Worker Bees) working together on AI jobs |
| **Honey** | The final answer delivered back to the client — the polished, combined result |
| **Nectar** | The initial question or task submitted by the client — the raw input |
| **Beekeeper** | The company or individual customer who pays for AI work to be done |

---

## How It Works

1. A **Beekeeper** submits a **Nectar** (question or task) to a **Hive**.
2. The **Queen Bee** splits the Nectar into small **Sub-tasks**.
3. Each **Worker Bee** processes one Sub-task using their local AI model.
4. The **Queen Bee** combines all answers into **Honey** (the final result).
5. The **Honey** is delivered back to the **Beekeeper**.
